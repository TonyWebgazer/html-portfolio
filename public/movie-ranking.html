The Best Movies According to Anthony

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bad Taste

Great comedy sci-fi film, made by Peter Jackson, before he was famous. Zero budget, unknown actors, and DIY special effects.

The Terminator

One of the greatest 80s sci-fi, full of A.I. paronoia. One of Cameron's, and Arnie's, greatest films.

Fight Club

A film about trying to fill the emptiness of a life with no meaning, where zombie consumerism isn't the answer, but neither is the anarchic rejection of modern society.
